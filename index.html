Welcome To
MyDream Public School

In the last 20 years, MyDream Public school has grown by leaps and bounds to take its place among the top schools of central India. With five branches, and an enrollment of over 7500+ students, SPS also enjoys the distinction of being one of the largest schools in central India. SPS, one of the best School in Bhopal, has a vibrant atmosphere for proper teaching and learning and a faculty rich in talent and experience. As enterprise and endeavour are its watchwords, the school always keeps an eye on new educational initiatives, so that the road to excellence, on which the institution has already launched itself, becomes smoother, more navigable and more accessible. Quality enhancement is its mission - it tries to create a win-win situation for its students with a personalized and positive approach towards education.
